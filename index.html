your guide for the capital of the province of North Holland

Haarlem is located on the Spaarne River and in the Zuid-Kennemerland region, it has a surface of 32.09 km² and a population of 165,650 people, making it the largest city in North Holland after Amsterdam and the thirteenth largest municipality in the Netherlands. In such a large city it is easy to get lost through the convoluted streets, especially if you arean expat or turist. Here on this website you can easily find useful locations for your stay in Haarlem.

Dry cleaners

It is possible during the time a person is staying in Haarlem to have to clean his clothes but his place doesn’t have a washing machine. For the people in this kind of problem there is the option of going to a dry cleaner in Haarlem. There is a surprising amount of dry cleaners in the city and some of them are open even Sundays.

The age of the internet

            Every so many years, the world goes through a period of rapid development. Generations that are only a few decades a part can barely recognize each other.

            Over the past few decades, the world hit the most significant growth period ever, the age of the internet . The creation of the internet changed our behaviors and interests, but it also created new problems.

            Access to constant information flooded the individual. Instant gradification is now the standard, attention spans have dropped at a new low. Many of the effects of the internet are being discussed. One area however, that is rarely talked about is how we adapted to express and share meaning through media and art.
